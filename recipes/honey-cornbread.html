Honey Cornbread

Description

This sweet and crumbly cornbread is a great side dish for most any meal. Or have it as a satisfying snack.

Easy and fast to make, with practice you can whip this together in just over half an hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 C).
  2. Lightly grease a 9x9 inch baking pan.
  3. In a large bowl, stir together flour, cornmeal, sugar, and baking powder.
  4. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients.
  5. Add the cream, oil, honey, and eggs; stir to combine.
  6. Pour batter into prepared baking pan.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center of the pan comes out clean.
  8. Cut and eat while still hot or let stand to cool

Tip: Enjoy hot with butter for extra deliciousness.
